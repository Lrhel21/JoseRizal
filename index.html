Rizal Law , Republic Act No. 1425

RA No. 1425

AN ACT TO INCLUDE IN THE CURRICULA OF ALL PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS, COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES COURSES ON THE LIFE, WORKS AND WRITINGS OF JOSE RIZAL, PARTICULARLY HIS NOVELS NOLI ME TANGERE AND EL FILIBUSTERISMO, AUTHORIZING THE PRINTING AND DISTRIBUTION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

Our mission

At our website, we strive to shed light on the remarkable life and enduring legacy of Jose Rizal, Through our research, analysis, and thoughtful presentation, we aim to inspire a deeper understanding and appreciation for Rizal's Works, Ideologies, and his imapact on the philippines and the world.

Book: "The Life and Works of Jose Rizal"

Our website draws inspiraxtion and knowledge from the book "The Life and Works of Jose Rizal". This seminal work provides valuable insight into Rizal's Journey, His pivotal role in the Philippine History, and his intellectual contributions. We explore the books content and augment it with additional resources and interpretations,making it accessible to a wider audience.
